Rate Engines Explained
All values are aggregated based upon the engine settings included in the analysis.
  • Rate Engines:
Active rate engines included in the calculated rate formula.
  • All-In Rate:
Calculated rate including fuel surcharges and/or accessorials.
  • Line Haul Rate:
Calculated rate not including fuel surcharges and/or accessorials.
  • Reports:
Total number of rate reports included in the analysis for this engine. (Note: For Chainalytics engines, a dash "-" will be displayed for reports since Chainalytics generates a single rate that is benchmarked using numerous industry cost drivers).
  • Avg Fuel Cost:
Average of the fuel surcharges that were included when rates were captured. For market rates that do not include a fuel surcharge, Truckstop.com will use a default $0.05 fuel scale.
